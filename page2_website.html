The Adventures of Nosnej is a 2D platformer game inspired by games like Super Mario and Doodle Champion Island Games. It includes this website, a demo game and an animation. The Adventures of Nosnej follows a player character through a pixelated world of intruige and mystery.

The player must collect coins and items to up their point score. Gold coins are the most common collectable but as you explore the world you can find a variety of precious stones and weapons, even flowers.

On this website you will find: the demo game, an animated trailer and pages related to the assets used in building the game, as well as proposed assets for the final edition.

Help Nosnej find his way through this new world he has been sucked into! Collect items, spend coins and explore the world he has found himself in. Hopefully you'll find a way out...
